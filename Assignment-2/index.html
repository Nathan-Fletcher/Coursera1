The Best Flavor of Top Ramen

Beef

Beef is the clear favorite here. It's the age-old ramen tradition and there's a reason it has thrived for so long. Go buy yourself some beef Top Ramen.

Chicken

Chicken will never be able to top the classic taste of beef, but if you're feeling tired of the same ol' flavor, it's a tasty alternative.

Shrimp

Who the hell goes to the store for shrimp ramen? We're not mad at you, just a little disappointed.

Opinion provided by my girlfriend. She eats a lot of Top Ramen.
